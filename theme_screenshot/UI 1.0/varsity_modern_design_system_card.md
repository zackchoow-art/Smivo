# Design System: Varsity Modern (US Campus Hub)

## 1. Creative North Star: "The Digital Quad"
Capturing the kinetic energy of a modern US university. Bold, editorial, and high-energy. Focus on trust through sophistication and a visual-first approach.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary (Electric Blue):** `#2D5BFF` — Represents energy and digital pulse. Used for primary CTAs and active states.
- **Surface:** `#FAF8FE` — A pristine, lilac-tinted white for the foundation.
- **Surface Container Lowest:** `#FFFFFF` — Pure white for elevated interactive cards.
- **Surface Container Low:** `#F4F3FA` — Subtle off-white for secondary background areas.
- **On Surface (Charcoal):** `#2B2A51` — High-contrast navy/charcoal for all headlines and primary text.
- **On Surface Variant:** `#646681` — Muted slate for labels and secondary text.
- **Alert (Red):** `#EF4444` — For notification badges and critical alerts.

## 3. Typography (Plus Jakarta Sans)
Geometric clarity with a bold, editorial scale.
- **Display (Hero):** 36px / 700 (Bold) / #2B2A51
- **Headline:** 24px / 700 (Bold) / #2B2A51
- **Title (Cards):** 18px / 600 (Semi-Bold) / #2B2A51
- **Body:** 14px / 400 (Regular) / #2B2A51
- **Label:** 12px / 600 (Semi-Bold) / Uppercase / #646681

## 4. Layout & Components
- **Base Unit:** 8px
- **Corner Radius:** 16px (Medium) for cards and primary buttons; 8px for smaller elements.
- **Primary Button:** Solid Electric Blue background with White text.
- **Surface Architecture:** No-line rule. Use tonal shifts and negative space instead of 1px borders.
- **Glassmorphism:** Use `backdrop-blur: 20px` for floating navigation and top-level overlays.
