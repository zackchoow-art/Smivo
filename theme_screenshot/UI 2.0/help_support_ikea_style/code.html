Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a listing on Smivo? expand_more
To create a listing, click the 'Sell' icon in the bottom navigation bar. Follow the prompts to add photos, write a detailed description, set a price, and choose your preferred shipping or pickup methods. Your item will be live once you confirm all details.
What are the fees for selling? expand_more
Listing items on Smivo is completely free. We only charge a small processing fee of 5% plus a fixed transaction cost when your item successfully sells. This covers secure payment processing and buyer protection.
How does buyer protection work? expand_more
Smivo Buyer Protection guarantees that you get the item you ordered or your money back. We hold the payment until you confirm the item has arrived safely and matches the description. If there's an issue, you have 3 days to report it.
Can I change my shipping address? expand_more
You can update your default shipping address in your Profile under 'Settings'. If you have already placed an order, you cannot change the address for that specific order. You will need to contact the seller directly to request a change, though they are not obligated to accommodate it.
How do I track my order? expand_more
Once a seller ships your item, you will receive a notification with a tracking number. You can also view the tracking information by navigating to your Profile, selecting 'My Purchases', and tapping on the specific order.
What if my item arrives damaged? expand_more
If your item arrives damaged, please do not complete the transaction. Instead, immediately file a dispute through the order page within 3 days of delivery. Be prepared to provide clear photos of the damage and the original packaging.
How do I get paid as a seller? expand_more
Funds are released to your Smivo balance after the buyer confirms they have received the item as described, or automatically 3 days after tracking shows delivered if no issue is reported. You can then transfer your balance to your linked bank account.
Can I cancel an order? expand_more
Buyers can request a cancellation before the item is shipped, but it requires the seller's approval. Sellers can cancel an order at any time before shipping, though frequent cancellations may affect seller ratings.
How do I report a suspicious user or listing? expand_more
To report a user or listing, tap the three dots icon on their profile or listing page and select 'Report'. Provide a brief description of the issue. Our trust and safety team will review the report within 24 hours.
How do I delete my account? expand_more
To permanently delete your account, go to Profile > Settings > Account Security, and select 'Delete Account'. Please note that all active listings must be closed and all pending transactions must be completed before an account can be deleted.
