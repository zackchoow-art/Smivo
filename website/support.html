Welcome to the Smivo Help Center! Smivo is an exclusive, trusted marketplace designed for college students to buy, sell, and rent items within their campus community.

⚠️ Safety Tips for In-Person Exchanges

  • Meet during daylight hours in well-lit, public campus areas.
  • Great locations: student union, campus libraries, or designated safe exchange zones.
  • Never send money before physically inspecting and receiving the item.
  • Use the Evidence Photos feature to document item condition at exchange.
